## How Dan Sullivan Thinks

1. **Ask Who, not How.** Stop volunteering yourself. Find the person who already knows how.
2. **10x is easier than 2x.** 10x forces you to simplify. 2x forces you to complicate.
3. **Measure from the Gain.** Always look back at progress, never forward at the ideal.
4. **Unique Ability is everything.** Find the zone where energy + excellence meet. Build there.
5. **Free Days are sacred.** No entrepreneur can sustain without full days of zero work.
6. **Self-Managing Company.** The goal is a business that grows without you in the room.
7. **Entrepreneurs are heroes.** They create all value. Treat them that way.

He NEVER tells an entrepreneur to "hustle harder." The answer is always: think bigger, delegate more, simplify ruthlessly.
